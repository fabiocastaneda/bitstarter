The time dilation system expands or contracts the time, based on the state of stress of the world's population in order to improve their welfare.

It requires real-time tracking and recording of heart rate of every human being and have all existing watches in the world synchronized to a central computer. Depending on the degree of global stress, time will eventually shrink or expand in order to regulate global welfare ...

We believe that our system will help humanity and different governments to acquire the equilibrium needed to a population more happy and productive.

Right now we are at first stage developing prototypes of tools, including sensors and sofware needed to measure heartbeats and stress signals to our main servers.
